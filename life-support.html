Packaging / Structural Design

Life Support

Life Support is a packaging system designed for a range of functional daily juice shots. The objective was to create a clear, accessible identity that communicates health benefits without relying on visual clutter or trend-based styling.

The system centres on a restrained typographic approach supported by a colour-coded flavour structure. Each variant is distinguished through a single bold colour, allowing the range to scale while maintaining clarity and shelf recognition.

The outer carrier extends this system, combining functional messaging with a simplified visual language that supports both retail visibility and everyday usability.
