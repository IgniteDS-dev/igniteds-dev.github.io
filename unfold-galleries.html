Unfold Exhibition

Unfold is a poster series that explores design structure and typography. Each piece responds to its location, with Paris and Marrakesh represented through both form and language.

The Eiffel Tower is translated into a dense field of linear forms, shifting it from a recognisable object into a graphic system. In contrast, the Marrakesh design represents the beauty of fabrics and jewels of the souks, creating a saturated field of colour and texture.

The word “Unfold” is set in the language of its environment, acting as a dominant typographic element that cuts through this complexity to create contrast and legibility at scale.
